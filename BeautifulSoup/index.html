ABOUT

A little about me.

  • MongoDB

PROJECTS

PERI Montessori

This website was made for PERI Montessori, Dibrugarh center, a teachers training institute. The website is built my vanillaJS and bootstrap at the frontend; nodeJS/expressJS at the backend and MongoDB atlas for database. The site helps the owner to maintain student records and provide regular information to the students and general public. It also contains a file sharing application by which students and faculty can exchange notes and assignments.

SSC Classes

This website was made for S.S.C. Classes, a private tutorial center. The website is built my vanillaJS and bootstrap at the frontend; nodeJS/expressJS at the backend and MongoDB atlas for database. It also contains a mark down for a blog where the owner can post regularly. Also there is a job and career section where career related information is published. The site helps the owner to maintain student records and manage content by simply logging in.
